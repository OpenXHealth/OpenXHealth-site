🚀 About OpenXHealth

OpenXHealth is a open-source community focused on applying AI and machine learning to solve real-world healthcare problems.We bring together learners, developers, and researchers to build impactful health-tech projects from disease detection models to health data dashboards.

Whether you're building your first ML model or deploying real-world solutions, OpenXHealth is your space to collaborate, contribute, and grow.
